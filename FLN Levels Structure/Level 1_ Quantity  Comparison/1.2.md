# Sub-Level 1.2

### **Description**

This sub-level provides the gentlest introduction to quantity comparison, using very small numbers and highly distinct visual aids to help students grasp the basics of equal matching and more/less.

### **Sections**

#### **Section A: Equal Quantity**

* Match single objects or tiny groups (1 to 3 objects) of identical items.

#### **Section B: More/Less**

* Compare two highly contrasting groups (e.g., 3 objects vs 1 object) and circle the one with more or less.

### **Examples**

* Match equal objects: (Group of 2 balls) ↔ (Group of 2 balls)
* Circle the group with more items: (3 balloons) vs (1 balloon)
